Check Out Medicall's Amazing Features:

Submit Prescription

For the safety use of medicine for all, A member of the pharmacy team will check the doctor’s information, medication/dose, indications from the prescriber, and quantity of the script. This allows the consumer to submit a prescription for verification and assessment of a healthcare professional.

Read More

Drug alerts

In this new feature of Medicall App Consumers can now "favorite" their chosen medicine and be informed of its status and availability,(Out of stock, New Stock, etc.) .This information will allow the buyer to personalize the arrangement of medicines since the favorites will be shown first.

Read More

Wishlist System

This system will allow the customer to be able to keep track of all the medicine that they are supposed to buy, how many and how much it would all cost. This would also include the individual price of the medicine and then the total price depending on the amount of choosing.

Read More

Medication Tracker

Customer may be able to set up the date, time, amount and number of repetitions and then allow the app to create a cycle that each and every time would remind the customer to take their medicine, and they could set the number of times they would be reminded which would be set by the customer depending on the amount of medicine they have or depending on the repetition their doctor have set for them. It also allows personalization, depending on the user's preference.

Read More

Suggestive Alerts

When the customer is buying medicine, the app will show suggestions depending on what type of medicine the customer is going to buy. After selecting a medicine, the app will suggest either a more trusted brand (more pieces sold) or a cheaper brand depending on what type of brand the customer chooses to buy, to provide easier navigation, instead of searching the whole list for the same medicine, but for a different brand, and also to open up options for them.
