Hello there! We are the All-Nighters, the country's most accessible health care distributor online. Our team was created during this pandemic to expand ABC's services in this trying times.

We assure that the medications we provide to our customers are new and in best quality. Medicall have features that are simple, convenient, and useful for our customers. Our team secures the safety of the product and timely response to the customer's needs.

We are All-Nighters. Medicall. One call away.

See our Services

Mission

Our mission is to deliver, both literally and figuratively, patient-centered treatment that is of the highest quality, service, and accessibility.

Vision

We strive to build a community where all users can receive healthcare during this pandemic and improve their overall well-being. We want to be a trusted partner in the community, as well as agents of positive change, for our consumers.

Values

The following are the Medicall's values:

To the people we serve:

  • Always put the customer first. We are committed to providing excellent patient care.
  • Treat everyone with dignity and respect.
  • We offer patient-centered care. If you order something from us, we guarantee that you will receive it in any way, shape, or form, regardless of who you are or what your needs are.

To the community:

  • We are dedicated to helping the community and giving open access to Medicall to all members of the community. Medicall is not just a service or a brand, but a movement.
  • Our services are current and relevant, and we are willing to adapt to any world-changing event in order to keep our services running.
  • Our clients will become constructive influences in the community and contribute to the health of others if we instill confidence in them.

To those affiliated with us:

  • Our top priority is the safety of our workforce, with pandemic or not.
  • We are fair to set measurable and attainable goals to keep all of us motivated in achieving said goals.
  • We are delighted in making time to properly address the needs and requests of each and every one in the workforce.
